Established in 2010, SUHAIM started a missionary endeavour…

To be in the heart of every Muslim’s effort of learning and mastering Quran recitation and Islamic teachings.

Developing Islamic ICT Brands for all ages

We work towards developing brands and interactive materials with a dedicated development team of editors, designers, engineers and like minded partners.

Distribution of Islamic Materials in SEA

SUHAIM participates in distribution of high quality Islamic materials from our partners that are impactful for the society.

For Our Children


We aspire to keep up the sparkles in our little ones, to excite their daily experiences as lil’ Muslims. We transform what could be dry theoretical content into interactive Magical Moments for our Children. When we give our children the necessary tools to succeed, they can succeed beyond their wildest dreams, fid dunya wal akhirah, in shaa Allah! Children brands: Tap Genius, Siri Buku Islamik Upin Ipin, Monte Mukmin, Muslimazing!

Values Close to Heart

 

Passion

Nothing is more heartwarming than getting to know that a Grandmom had use the Stylus Sensor to recite her Quran daily and now for the 4th year; A new Muslim mastering his solah by the audio reference from the touch technology, batches of students in Quran Centres benefitting from the publications and audio; and little children memorizing a new surah today. Alhamdulillah, we are heading on for more…

 

Integrity

Simple in our approach, transparent and reliable in our relation. In our product brands, we work to ensure development and operation stems from genuine resources and adheres to our ethical principles.

 

Quality

For what its worth, we seek pleasure and will go the extra mile to offer high quality, durable items at affordable price. And to top it off, good customer service in shaa Allah. 

 

Strive for Barakah

The family of SUHAIM strive to ensure the highest ethical standards when we serve, innovate and perform.
